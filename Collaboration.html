Collaboration in the future.

Relations refer to the connections among the members of a group or the social network within a group. Members in a group are linked to one another at varying levels.
The RSGAC aims to enhance the relations between its members through focus areas and initiatives that will increase collaboration and cooperation in all fields between member states, create cohesive agendas, work towards common goals, and promote mutual growth throughout the council.
The RSGAC's existence will not only increase cooperation through its key focus areas, but also stimulate growth between members. This can be achieved through regular council meetings and progress updates, mutual initiatives that boost cooperation, maintenance of strong dialogue between member representatives, and policies that mutually benefit all members.
Collaboration is critical to the council, and therefore we value partnerships with blocs and organizations from all around the world. A collaborative international community allows the entire world to develop, learn, and prosper. The RSGAC will work with regional and international organizations, blocs, and commissions from all around the world.
The Supreme Council, comprising the kings and heads of states of the member states of the council, shall be the highest authority of the council. The Supreme Council shall be chaired in rotation for each term pursuant to the alphabetical order of the names of member states. As an exception, the council shall be chaired for the first term by the host country. The Supreme Council shall regularly convene once a year and may hold extraordinary meetings where necessary. The meetings shall be valid if attended by two-thirds of the council members.
