Environment In The Future.

Changing the perception of the Environment in the future

Toshiba Group's long-standing Basic Commitment is "Committed to People, Committed to the Future." This statement expresses the company's unwavering dedication to contributing to the development of society through their business. Since their founding, Toshiba has aimed to combine the power of invention with their expertise to tackle increasingly complex social issues and create a better world. Under their mid-term business plan, they will provide unique services that are highly reliable and state-of-the-art technologies to help solve social issues while contributing to the achievement of the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) and enhancing their corporate value. Toshiba Group regards environmental initiatives as one of their top priority tasks in corporate management and will promote such initiatives closely linked to the company-wide business plan.
In recent years, various environmental issues such as climate change, depletion of energy and resources, have intensified and threaten the safe and secure lives of future generations. The adoption of the Paris Agreement*1 in 2015 has accelerated the movement toward carbon neutrality in each country. Therefore, companies must recognize the importance of climate change from a long-term perspective and proactively respond to achieve carbon neutrality. Countries worldwide are also trying to address issues such as the transition to a circular economy, marine plastics, water resources, and biodiversity conservation. As a result, society's interest in such issues is on the rise. Meanwhile, the dissemination of the SDGs, the expansion of ESG investment, and other movements involving corporate management aimed at sustainability overall are gaining momentum.
