Who Are The Members of RSGA ?

Members
Sudan

Sudan is a country located at the crossroads of Sub-Saharan Africa and the Middle East, stretching across the Red Sea

Saudi Arabia

Saudi Arabia is a sparsely populated kingdom located in the Middle East, extending across most of the northern and central Arabian Peninsula. Despite its youth as a nation, Saudi Arabia is heir to a rich history.

Egypt

Egypt, officially known as the Arab Republic of Egypt, is an incredibly fascinating country that boasts an abundance of history, culture, cuisine, religion, and diverse geography

Somalia

Somalia is located at the easternmost part of Africa, occupying a strategic position between sub-Saharan Africa, Arabia, and southwestern Asia.

yemen

Yemen is a country located in the southwestern corner of the Arabian Peninsula. It is a mostly mountainous and arid region, but there are large areas with sufficient rainfall to make agriculture successful

Eritrea

Eritrea is a country located on the Horn of Africa with a coastal location on the Red Sea. Its name reflects the importance of its history and culture, as Mare Erythraeum is Latin for "Red Sea."

Djibouti

Djibouti is a small but strategically important country located on the northeast coast of the Horn of Africa. It is situated on the Bab el Mandeb Strait, which separates the Red Sea from the Gulf of Aden.

Jordan

Jordan is a young nation situated on an ancient land that has been home to many civilizations throughout history. Its borders are separated from ancient Palestine by the Jordan River, and the region has played a prominent role in biblical history.
