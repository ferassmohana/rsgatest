About The State

Egypt, officially known as the Arab Republic of Egypt, is an incredibly fascinating country that boasts an abundance of history, culture, cuisine, religion, and diverse geography. With its unique characteristics, Egypt offers something special for every visitor who has the opportunity to experience this magnificent place.
Egyptian history is truly unparalleled and is considered one of the greatest inheritances of human civilization. However, Egypt is much more than just its ancient pyramids and monuments that date back thousands of years. It is a country that is alive and full of life, and undoubtedly a place that every person should visit at least once in their lifetime.
Located in the Gulf of Aqaba, Egypt has coastlines on both the Mediterranean and the Red Sea, and the Nile River flows through the entirety of the country. Egypt shares borders with Libya, the Gaza Strip, Sudan, and Palestine. The Western Desert, which makes up about two-thirds of Egypt's land area, is a part of the Sahara Desert and is situated on the west side of the Nile River.
